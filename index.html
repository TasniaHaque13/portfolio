TASNIA HAQUE

Graduate Planner specializing in Environmental Engineering & Planning

Hello! I am Tasnia Haque. I have completed my undergrad in Urban and Regional Planning from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Bangladesh in March 2024. My personal and professional interests are driven by a desire to contribute meaningful work for the earth, the environment, and communities. My academic interests focus on applying geospatial and data-driven approaches to address challenges at the intersection of urban systems, environmental change, and climate adaptation.

My research focuses on the intersection of climate processes, environmental hazards, land-use dynamics, urban resilience, sustainability, transportation systems, inclusive placemaking, and public health. I am particularly interested in understanding the dynamic relationships among these domains and how they collectively influence ecological and urban systems. My goal is to contribute toward improving ecological balance and creating more inclusive, resilient urban environments.

Undergrad Thesis

Title: Coastal Vulnerability Assessment to Climate Change Impact: A Case study of Bagerhat, Khulna and Satkhira District

The aim is to comprehensively evaluate the coastal vulnerability condition by integrating the spatial pattern of exposure, sensitivity, and adaptive capacity in the study area. The key objective is to develop a coastal vulnerability index at the upazila level and ranking areas in terms of their susceptibility to coastal hazards.
